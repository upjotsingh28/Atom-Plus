# Atom Plus

Willing to get past your competitors ? Wanna score the highest ?

● Atom Plus provides you with the previous year question papers for Maharshi Dayanand University (M.D.U.) and that with the solutions as well !!

● So that you can simply study the pattern of the questions that have been asked previously and then study accordingly.

● Doing so, will get you an upper hand in the exams and you can easily score the highest and who knows maybe you can secure university rank as well.

● Also you can use out app to check your MDU results simply by going to the results tab.

● Also we really feel good helping students and love to see the excel in their career and so we are also announcing internships support for 8th semester students and project support as well in the upcoming updates, Hurray :)
